Smart Gloves for Mute People

In this project the hand gesture which is also known as sign language will be converted into voice for mute people. The system incorporates a text to speech conversion block that interrupts the signs that match i.e. text to speech out. It was ranked top third in the VIT-AP Engineering Clinics Expo.
